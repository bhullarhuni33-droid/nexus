FAQ

Got questions?
We got answers.

Is NEXUS safe to install?
+
Yes 100%. Windows shows a warning for all new apps that aren't from the Microsoft Store yet — this is normal. Click "More info" then "Run anyway" to install safely. Our code is open source and available on GitHub for anyone to check.
Does NEXUS store my conversations?
+
Your conversations are stored locally on your device only — never on our servers. Screenshots taken for screen vision are processed and immediately discarded. We never sell your data.
What does "sees your screen" mean?
+
When you ask NEXUS something like "what's wrong with my code?" it takes a screenshot of your screen, sends it to an AI vision model to understand what's on screen, then gives you a specific answer based on what it sees. Screenshots are never stored.
Does it work without internet?
+
NEXUS needs internet to process your questions — it uses cloud AI models to give you smart answers. However it works on any internet connection including mobile hotspot.
What is the difference between Free and Pro?
+
Free users get 10 questions per day — enough for casual use. Pro users get unlimited questions, priority response speed, and early access to new features. All for just ₹299/month.
Which Windows versions are supported?
+
NEXUS works on Windows 10 and Windows 11. Mac and Linux versions are coming soon.
